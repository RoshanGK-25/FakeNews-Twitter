Welcome To Social Miner Using Twitter

 

As people are free and internet is closer to them,so they share their opinions using social networking sites such as Facebook,Twiiter,etc. Twitter messages(tweets)are used to share the opinions and sentiments about surrounding world. So it is a challenge to study public opinion. In this we took twitter data for sentimet analysis.The sentiment analysis is done on a per-tweet basis. The words in each tweet are compared with those in other tweets that have been already labeled as “positive” or “negative”. After that,the algorithm then judges whether the text in tweet is positive or negative based on the likelihood for each possibility.
