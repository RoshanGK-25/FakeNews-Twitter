About Us

Data collection from social media: Our system collect information from social networking sites such as Facebook ,Twitter ,Linkedin ,Instagram etc. Publically such information is not available, so we use corresponding APIâ€™s for that.
Document Preprocessing: This can be achieved by cleaning and simplifying the data by applying spell correction ,punctuation handling ,tokenization etc .results in removal of noise from document.
Maintained Dictionary: System also involves another input which is maintained dictionary, so that we can compare words from document to words in dictionary. This dictionary consists of all positive, negative related similar kind of words.
Word-Matching Technique :Word Matching technique is applied on words extracted from document. We use TF-IDF algorithm for word matching.
Classification of Words: After word-matching we classify the words into three categories: Positive Negative or neutral.
